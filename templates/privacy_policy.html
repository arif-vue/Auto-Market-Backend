AutoMarket Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 30, 2025

Information We Collect

AutoMarket collects the following information to provide marketplace integration services:

  • eBay account information (seller ID, store details)
  • Product listings and inventory data
  • Transaction history for listing management
  • Contact information for customer service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Manage and sync your product listings across marketplaces
  • Process inventory updates and order fulfillment
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Generate sales reports and analytics

Information Sharing

AutoMarket does not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With eBay and Amazon APIs as necessary to provide our services
  • When required by law or legal process
  • With your explicit consent

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data
  • Secure API connections with OAuth authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and user permission management

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

eBay Integration

When you connect your eBay account to AutoMarket:

  • We access your eBay seller account data through official eBay APIs
  • Your eBay credentials are never stored on our servers
  • We use OAuth tokens that can be revoked at any time
  • All eBay data synchronization follows eBay's Terms of Service
